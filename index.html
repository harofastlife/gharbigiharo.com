About Me

Nearly two decades of building technology that matters

Based in the heart of Los Angeles, I've dedicated my career to the intersection of technology and business innovation. My journey spans nearly two decades of creating software solutions that solve real problems for real people.

Currently, I'm channeling my expertise into the healthcare sector through AxiaASC, where we're developing next-generation software specifically designed for ambulatory surgery centers. Our mission is to harness the power of artificial intelligence to streamline operations, enhance patient outcomes, and free healthcare professionals to focus on what they do best: caring for patients.

I believe that the most impactful technology is invisible technology—solutions that work seamlessly in the background, making complex processes simple and giving time back to the people who use them.
