Los Angeles

Tech entrepreneurship in Southern California

Los Angeles is my home and the base for my entrepreneurial ventures. The city's unique blend of technology innovation, healthcare infrastructure, and diverse talent pool makes it an ideal location for building AxiaASC and pursuing opportunities at the intersection of AI and healthcare.

Why Los Angeles for Healthcare Tech

LA's tech ecosystem has matured significantly in recent years. While Silicon Valley remains the traditional startup hub, Los Angeles offers distinct advantages: proximity to major healthcare systems, a lower cost of living than the Bay Area, and access to a deep pool of creative and technical talent.

Southern California is home to over 600 ambulatory surgery centers—one of the highest concentrations in the country. This provides immediate market access for AxiaASC and enables direct relationships with the healthcare professionals we serve. Understanding their daily challenges firsthand shapes better products.

Deep Roots in Southern California

My roots in the LA area run deep. From my early education to building multiple technology ventures, Southern California has provided the foundation and community that supports entrepreneurial growth. The region's healthcare sector provides direct market access for AxiaASC, while its tech community offers the talent and partnerships needed to build world-class software.

Los Angeles has always been a city of reinvention, and its tech scene reflects that spirit. From entertainment technology to aerospace to healthcare innovation, LA entrepreneurs tackle ambitious problems. That energy drives me to build solutions that make a real difference in people's lives.

California's Innovation Ecosystem

Being based in Los Angeles connects me to California's broader tech and healthcare innovation ecosystem, from San Diego's biotech cluster to the Bay Area's AI research community. This geographic positioning enables valuable partnerships and keeps AxiaASC connected to the latest advances in both healthcare and AI.

California leads the nation in healthcare technology adoption, making it the ideal proving ground for new solutions. Success here provides a roadmap for expansion across the country. The state's stringent healthcare regulations also ensure that solutions built here meet the highest standards.
