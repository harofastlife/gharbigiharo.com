Medium Profile

Long-form articles and industry insights

Medium provides the ideal platform for sharing in-depth analysis and perspectives on the rapidly evolving landscape of healthcare technology and artificial intelligence. My articles explore practical applications of AI in business settings, particularly within the healthcare sector.

Writing serves as both a way to contribute to industry conversations and to crystallize my own thinking about complex topics. Each piece draws from real-world experience building technology companies over nearly two decades.

Topics covered range from implementation strategies for AI in healthcare settings to broader discussions about entrepreneurship and technology leadership. The goal is always to provide actionable insights rather than theoretical speculation.
